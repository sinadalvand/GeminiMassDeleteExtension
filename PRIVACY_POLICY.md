# Privacy Policy for Gemini Mass Delete

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **Gemini Mass Delete** (the "Extension") handles your data.

## 1. No Data Collection
The Extension **does not collect, store, track, or transmit** any personal data, browsing history, or user information. All operations are performed entirely locally within your browser.

## 2. Local Processing Only
Any data used by the Extension (such as selecting which chats to delete or storing temporary checkbox states) is processed strictly client-side on your device:
- The Extension interacts directly with the DOM on `https://gemini.google.com/`.
- Temporary selection states are stored locally in your browser's `localStorage` and are completely cleared when selection mode is canceled or when deletion is completed.
- No information is sent to the developer or any third-party servers.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The Extension operates on the Google Gemini website, which is subject to Google's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. The Extension does not share any of your Gemini chat content or account details with third parties.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions or feedback about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/sinadalvand/GeminiMassDeleteExtension).
